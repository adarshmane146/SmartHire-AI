ATS Compatibility Checker

Ensure your resume makes it past Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS). Most modern companies use these systems to automatically filter resumes before a human recruiter ever sees them. Maximize your chances of landing an interview.

How to pass the ATS filter:

  • Use standard fonts: like Arial, Calibri, or Inter.
  • Clear Formatting: Avoid complex columns, tables, or heavy graphics.
  • Keyword Matches: Include exact technical skills matching the job description.
  • File Type: Save and upload your resume strictly as a PDF document.
